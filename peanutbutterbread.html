Peanut Butter Bread

Adapted from the original recipe by The View From Great Island, this version has some additions. Never heard of peanut butter bread? Well it's essentially banana bread...but peanut butter flavour! I usually eat it all within two days.

Prep time: 15m Cook time: 50m Total time: 65m est.

Ingredients

  • 1 ¾ cups Gluten Free Flour Mix
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • ½ tsp Baking Soda
  • A dash of salt
  • ¾ cup Smooth Peanut Butter (I use Kraft)
  • ½ cup Brown Sugar
  • ¼ cup Olive or Canola Oil
  • 1 lukewarm Egg
  • 1 tsp Real Vanilla Extract
  • ¾ cup Milk or Buttermilk
  • 2 tbsp Cinnamon
  • 2 handfuls of Dairy Free Chocolate Chips
  • OPTIONAL: ½ cup Chopped Nuts

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a small bowl mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt together with either a whisk or fork.
  3. In large bowl beat peanut butter, sugar, oil, egg, and vanilla together until fully combined.
  4. Add the dry ingredients alternately into the large bowl alongside the milk. Once combined add 1 handful of chocolate chips and mix.
  5. Get a loaf pan and either line it with parchment paper or grease. Pour batter into the loaf pan, you can either spread it evenly around for a flat top or leave it as it falls for a more domed shape.
  6. Scatter a second, smaller, handful of chocolate chips in a line on top of the batter.
  7. Bake for 50-55 minutes depending on your oven, I usually check at 45 when using new ovens. The inside should be an almost banana bread consistancy, not wet but damp when done.
  8. You need to let the bread sit for a few minutes once you take it out. It'll be more crumbly when it first comes out, but I usually can't wait very long to get a section for tasting.
