Bakery Style Chocolate Chip Muffins

Adapted from She Loves Biscotti, these gluten-free, dairy-free muffins have a perfect bakery-style dome and tender crumb. The cinnamon sugar topping adds a delightful crunch!

Prep time: 15m Cook time: 20m Total time: 35m

Ingredients

Dry Ingredients:

  • 2 cups gluten-free flour blend
  • ½ cup brown sugar
  • 1½ teaspoons baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda
  • Dash of salt
  • ½ cup dairy-free chocolate chips

Liquid Ingredients:

  • 2 eggs (room temperature)
  • ¼ cup canola oil
  • 1 cup plant milk + 1 tbsp lemon juice (buttermilk substitute)
  • 1 teaspoon real vanilla extract

Optional Topping:

  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together all dry ingredients including chocolate chips.
  3. If making the topping, mix sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl and set aside.
  4. In a large bowl, whisk together eggs, oil, plant milk mixture, and vanilla.
  5. Add dry ingredients to wet ingredients and mix until just combined (don't overmix).
  6. Line a muffin pan with liners or parchment squares. Divide batter evenly among cups.
  7. Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar topping if using.
  8. Bake for 18-20 minutes until golden and a toothpick comes out clean.
  9. Cool in pan for 5 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack.
