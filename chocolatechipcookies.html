Coconut Oil Chocolate Chip Cookies

Adapted from the original recipe by Life As A Strawberry, this version reflects the way I usually bake them. They're an easy, one-bowl recipe that comes together quickly and uses ingredients we all usually have in our pantries, making them a go-to for a GF/DF treat!

Preptime: 15m Cook time: 25m Total time: 40m est

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup Melted Coconut Oil
  • 2 tsp Real Vanilla Extract
  • 2 Eggs
  • ¼ cup White Sugar
  • ¾ cup Brown Sugar
  • 2 ½ cups Gluten Free Flour Mix
  • A dash of Salt
  • 1 tsp Baking Soda
  • A handful of Dairy Free Chocolate Chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375.
  2. In a large bowl whisk together melted coconut oil, vanilla, eggs, and both sugars.
  3. After contents are combined, add in flour, salt, and baking soda until smooth.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop dough with a small cookie scoop onto your stone or baking sheet. They shouldn't spread much.
  6. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until they're browning.
