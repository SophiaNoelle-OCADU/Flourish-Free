Gluten-Free Flour Guide

Best Flours for Cookies

  • Rice flour blend for crispy edges
  • Mixed flour for chewy texture
  • Oat flour for soft-batch style

Best Flours for Muffins

  • Sorghum flour for texture
  • Cassava flour for moisture
  • Oat flour for softness

Avoid

  • Rice flour for bread
  • Coconut flour for cookies
  • Buckwheat flour for its strong earthy flavor and grittiness

Blends

  • Ideal gluten-free flour mix for cookies: Rice flour blend
  • Ideal gluten-free flour mix for muffins: Sorghum and oat flour blend
  • General gluten-free flour mix: Almond and rice flour blend

Egg Tips & Substitutes

Temperature Tips

  • Room temperature eggs for better binding
  • 30 minutes before baking
  • Never use cold eggs in batters

Substitutes

  • Flaxseed meal: 1 tbsp + 3 tbsp water = 1 egg
  • Chia seeds: 1 tbsp + 3 tbsp water = 1 egg
  • Applesauce: 1/4 cup = 1 egg (not for crispy textures)

Dairy-Free Milk Alternatives

Almond Milk

  • Nutty taste
  • Good protein content
  • Avoid sweetened or flavored versions

Oat Milk

  • Generally tasteless in baked goods
  • Great for breads
  • Natural sweetness

Coconut Milk

  • Rich and creamy
  • Perfect for pastries
  • Adds moisture

Buttermilk Substitutions

1 cup milk + 1 tbsp vinegar or lemon juice

1 cup milk + 1 tbsp melted butter
